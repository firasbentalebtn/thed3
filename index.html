Gameplay Modes

In the Dungeon Mode, players can send Heroes to the dungeons and have them battle monsters and bosses to claim Theerinium Coin (THED coins).

Mirage of infinity

An infinite dungeon where Theringars can battle endlessly at the cost of stamina. Stamina refills overtime or with stamina potions

Story Mode

To fight against the newly awakened ancient demon, Theringars take up arms and take the fight to the dungeons of chaos. The game tells a story of this conflict across 5 dungeons and 2 realms.

the pit of might and sorrow(PVP):

A realm unbeknownst to men’s consciousness where theringars can engage in 1VS1 duels and climb the ranks to glory, … and maybe uncover the mysteries of this god-forsaken place.

Frequently Asked Questions

what is theerineum Dungeons?

Theerineum Dungeons is a dungeon crawler, Play to earn game in which players can battle monsters in different modes or battle each other in a pvp style mode. The objective is to gather THED coins and earn a real time profit along the way. Our goal is to create a place where people can enjoy a fun game & earn income at the same time.

what character classes will be available in theerineum Dungeons?

Assasin:

A dual blade wielder that boosts incredible attack speed able to overwhelm the enemy with a flurry of slashes

Fighter:

A master of the Greatsword, the vanguard of the theringar, he can withstand a great deal of damage while decimating the front ranks of the enemy.

Mage:

A scholar of the arcane, that can dispatch of enemies from a safe  distance using Void arcane and through the use of light arcane create a  protective barrier.

Archer:

A skilled bowman who can annihilate the enemy with a hail of arrows, He can attack from a great range with fast rush of arrows.

what kind of gameplay modes that will be available in theerineum Dungeons:

Mainely there will be 3 Gameplay modes once the game is released: -An inifine Dungeon mode.
-A story mode.
-A PvP Arena Mode.

what is $THED :

The $THED token is used as the in-game currency. Every in-game transaction will be conducted using this token including in-game purchases.

is it possible to earn $THED token by playing the game:

Theerineum ecosystem allows players to make an income with THED  coin by:
  • Killing the monsters in the dungeon
  • Defeating the bosses at different levels.
  • Selling the NFT heroes and NFT gear on the game marketplace.
  • PvP ranking rewards.
  • Special in-game events.
  • Daily rewards.
